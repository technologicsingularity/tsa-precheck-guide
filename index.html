Frequently Asked Questions

How long does TSA PreCheck approval take?

Most applicants receive their Known Traveler Number (KTN) within 3-5 business days after their enrollment appointment. Some may receive it in as little as 24 hours.

Is TSA PreCheck worth it?

Absolutely. At just $78 for 5 years ($15.60/year), TSA PreCheck pays for itself after just a few trips. The time saved and stress reduction make it one of the best travel investments available.

Can I use TSA PreCheck on international flights?

TSA PreCheck covers the security screening process at US airports, including for international departures. However, for fast customs re-entry when returning to the US, you'll want Global Entry.

Does my credit card cover TSA PreCheck?

Many premium credit cards like Chase Sapphire Reserve, Amex Platinum, and Capital One Venture X offer TSA PreCheck or Global Entry fee credits. Check your card benefits before paying out of pocket.

What if I'm denied TSA PreCheck?

If denied, you can appeal the decision or apply for a waiver. Common reasons for denial include certain criminal convictions or immigration status issues. Most applicants are approved.
